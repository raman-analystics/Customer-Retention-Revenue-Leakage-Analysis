# Customer-Retention-Revenue-Leakage-Analysis

🔥 Problem Statement
Customer retention is one of the most critical drivers of long-term revenue in e-commerce businesses. However, many companies struggle with low repeat purchase rates, leading to significant revenue leakage.
👉 This project aims to analyze customer behavior, identify retention gaps, and uncover key drivers behind low repeat purchases.

⚡ Key Highlight
97% of customers do not return after their first purchase, leading to massive revenue loss and inefficient customer acquisition spending.

🎯 Objectives
* Analyze customer retention patterns using cohort analysis
* Identify drop-off points in the customer lifecycle
* Understand key factors influencing repeat purchases
* Provide actionable business recommendations to improve retention

🗂️ Dataset
* Source: Brazilian E-commerce Dataset (Olist)
* Data includes:
    * Orders
    * Customers
    * Products
    * Order items
    * Delivery information

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* SQL → Data cleaning & transformation
* Tableau → Data visualization & dashboard creation

📈 Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is structured into four key sections:
1. KPI Overview
* Average Lifetime Value
* Repeat Purchase Rate
* Average Order Value

2. Retention Analysis
🔹 Cohort Analysis
* Tracks customer retention over time
* Shows steep drop immediately after first purchase
🔹 Retention Curve
* Visualizes how retention declines month over month
* Identifies critical churn point

3. Root Cause Analysis
🔹 Customer Behavior by Category
* Significant variation in repeat rates across product categories
* Indicates product-driven retention behavior
🔹 Impact of Delivery Speed
* Faster delivery correlates with higher repeat purchases
* Slow delivery negatively impacts customer return rate

4. Revenue Distribution
* Revenue is heavily concentrated among a small group of repeat customers
* Majority of customers contribute low value

🔍 Key Insights
* Customer retention drops sharply immediately after the first purchase across all cohorts
* Only ~3% of customers return, indicating extremely low customer loyalty
* Revenue is concentrated among a small group of repeat customers
* Retention varies significantly across product categories
* Faster delivery improves repeat purchase likelihood

💡 Business Recommendations
1. Improve Post-Purchase Engagement
* Launch email/SMS campaigns within 3–7 days of first purchase
* Recommend relevant products to encourage second purchase

2. Incentivize Repeat Purchases
* Offer discounts or loyalty rewards on second order
* Introduce limited-time offers for returning customers

3. Optimize High-Performing Categories
* Focus marketing spend on categories with higher repeat rates
* Bundle products to increase repeat engagement

4. Improve Delivery Experience
* Reduce delivery time for better customer satisfaction
* Set accurate delivery expectations

5. Strengthen First-Time Customer Experience
* Improve onboarding and product experience
* Build trust through better communication and service

🚀 Business Impact
If implemented, these strategies can:
* Increase repeat purchase rate
* Reduce customer acquisition cost
* Improve customer lifetime value
* Drive sustainable revenue growth
